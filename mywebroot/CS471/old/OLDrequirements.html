CS471 Homework Instructions

The purpose of the written assignments are to help you understand and apply programming language concepts to practical (common) languages.  Therefore to pass this course you must have an average of 65% on the written assignments.  You are expected to turn in homework every week even if you can not complete every question. 

The purpose of the programming assignments are to help you learn, practice and internalize new programming paradigms.  Therefore to pass this course you must have an average of 65% on the Haskell and Prolog assignments.   You should document any problems with your code not meeting specifications.
