%5 accumProd(+Lst, RLst):  Given a list, Lst accumProd creates/checks that RLst is a list of the running product of all the numbers of  Lst from right to left. 
%i.e.

 %   ?- accumProd([3,2,5,2],R).
 %  R = [60, 20, 10, 2]
 %   ?- accumProd([3,4,5,6,0.5],R).
 %   R = [180, 60, 15, 3, 0.5]
