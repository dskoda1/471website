    functor(?Term, ?Functor, ?Arity)
        Succeeds if Term is a term with functor Functor and arity Arity. If Term is a variable it is unified with a new term holding only variables. functor/3 silently fails on instantiation faults (13) If Term is an atom or number, Functor will be unified with Term and arity will be unified with the integer 0 (zero).
