    3. [1pt]The cause of the limitation is the accumulation of pending multiplications, which is inherent in the way power is defined— each multiplication has to wait until its right argument has been evaluated.
      4. One way to eliminate the multiplication delay is to convert the function’s definition to a tail-recursive implementation:

      > powerT :: Integer -> Integer -> Integer
      > powerT a b = trp b 1

      >      trp n p = if (n==0) then p else trp (n-1) (a*p)
